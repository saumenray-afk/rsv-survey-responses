💡 QUICK DECISION GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: "Should I use the mobile app or paper surveys?"
A: Mobile app (PWA) is HIGHLY recommended because:
   - Faster data collection
   - No manual data entry
   - Fewer errors
   - Better response rates
   - Professional appearance
   - Automatic backup
   
Q: "How many responses do I need?"
A: Minimum 100, Recommended 150-200 for:
   - Statistical reliability
   - Segment analysis
   - Robust projections

Q: "How long will collection take?"
A: Plan for 2-3 weeks with reminders

Q: "What if people don't have smartphones?"
A: Options:
   - Set up tablet in clubhouse
   - Print CSV as paper survey
   - Assistance from family members
   - Most residents have smartphones today

Q: "Is my data secure?"
A: Yes, data stored in PRIVATE GitHub repository
   - Only you have access
   - HTTPS encrypted
   - No third-party access
