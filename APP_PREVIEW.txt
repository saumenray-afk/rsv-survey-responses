═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Resident opens app
   ↓
2. Fills villa number, name, phone
   ↓
3. Completes 16 choice sets
   ↓
4. Clicks Submit
   ↓
5. Data sent to GitHub repository
   ↓
6. Also saved locally as backup
   ↓
7. Success screen shown with Response ID
   ↓
8. Data appears in GitHub responses folder
